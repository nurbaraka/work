Hello! I'm Harper. 👋 In 2018, I switched career from hospitality to graphic design, and in 2021, I transitioned to web and app design. I'm skilled in UI design, wireframing, and prototyping. I also enjoy getting involved in front-end development, where I turn clients' ideas and our solutions into successful digital products.

As a self-taught learner, most of my knowledge comes from books and the internet. Inspired by books such as "The Design of Everyday Things" and "User Friendly," I'm just getting started with HCI, aiming to build a strong foundation in UX design.

When I'm not entirely obsessed with tweaking pixels, you can find me hiking through scenic trails, reading a literary novel, or getting lost in a podcast on a long walk.
